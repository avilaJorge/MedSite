Radiation Oncologists

Our faculty includes 28 Radiation Oncologists who practice at 7 Treatment Centers throughout San Diego and beyond and participate in more than 25 multi-disciplinary tumor boards. Nineteen are primarily clinically-focused while 9 have a clinical/basic science focus. Ten were named Top Doctor in the 2018 San Diego Magazine.

 

Medical Physicists

Our Medical Physics Division is comprised of 18 Medical Physicists who are vital members of the clinical team ensuring high quality treatment of all our patients. Thirteen physicists are primarily clinically-focused while 5 are involved with new technology development and research.

 

Affiliated Faculty

Thirteen physicians, physicists, veterinary oncologists, computer scientists and others hold faculty positions in our Department and are involved in patient care, clinical and translational research, education and outreach.
