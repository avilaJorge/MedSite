Dr. Monica Khanna is an Associate Professor and the Medical Director of the Eisenhower Bighorn Radiation Oncology Center at Eisenhower Lucy Curci Cancer Center, an Affiliate of the UC San Diego Health Cancer Network. She joined the RMAS faculty as a Voluntary Associate Professor in 2017.

A respected and experienced clinical Radiation Oncologist with over 20 years of experience, Dr. Khanna has been recognized as a Top Doctor by the Palm Springs Life Magazine as well as a Top Doctor by Castle Connolly. She is a recipient of Soroptimist’s International Award for A Doctor Making a Difference and has received the City of Palm Desert’s Athena Award. In past years, she has Chaired the Eisenhower Medical Center Cancer Committee and served on the Board of Directors of the Desert Cancer Foundation. In 2017, she was recognized as a Spirit of the Mission Awardee at the American Cancer Society’s Desert Spirit Gala for her long time contributions to the community.
