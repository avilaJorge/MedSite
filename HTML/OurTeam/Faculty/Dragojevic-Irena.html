Dr. Dragojevic is an Associate Director of the Division of Medical Physics & Technology overseeing the Satellite Clinics.

She is based at the South Bay Treatment Facility and currently supervises the physics programs in South Bay, 4S Ranch and Encinitas. Her clinical projects include optimizing quality assurance, treatment planning, clinical work flow and efficiency at each of the centers. She also serves as a liaison between the Satellites and the main center in La Jolla.
