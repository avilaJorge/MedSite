Dr. Visudha Lingareddy is an Associate Professor and practices at the Eisenhower Bighorn Radiation Oncology Center at Eisenhower Lucy Curci Cancer Center, an Affiliate of the UC San Diego Health Cancer Network. She joined the RMAS faculty as a Voluntary Associate Professor in 2019.

Prior to coming to Eisenhower Medical Center, Dr. Lingareddy served as Medical Director for the Radiation Oncology Program at Edward Hospital in Naperville IL. She was active in clinical trial and oversaw the high-dose-rate (HDR) brachytherapy program for breast and gynecologic cancer patients. She was very active in her community serving on the Du Page County American Cancer Society Executive Board and the Y-ME Advisory Board. In 2003, she was named a Fellow by the American College of Radiation Oncology (ACRO).
