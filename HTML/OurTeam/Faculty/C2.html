CHANG CHANG, PhD

ASSOCIATE PROFESSOR

UCSD PROFILE
  • Birthplace | Taipei, Taiwan
  • BS Electrical Engineering | National Taiwan University 1996
  • MEng Electrical Engineering | Cornell University 1997
  • Residency | University of Pennsylvania 2012
  • Board Certification | American Board of Radiology 2012

Dr. Chang is an associate professor in the Division of Medical Physics and Technology and serves as the Director of Physics for the California Protons Cancer Therapy Center. Prior to joining UCSD, Dr. Chang was the Director of Physics at the Texas Center for Proton Therapy where he leads the physics and dosimetry effort from commissioning to treating patients. Working with the physicians and clinical teams, Dr. Chang has directed the implementation of a comprehensive clinical program for proton PBS motion mitigation in the thorax and upper abdomen. Dr. Chang has also given many invited talks and presentations in various national and international conferences on proton therapy.

Dr. Chang enjoys spending time with his family, running, swimming, and programming. He is also currently looking into surfing.
