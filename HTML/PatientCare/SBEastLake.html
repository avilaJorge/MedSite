Our Center

The facility is equipped with 2 Varian Linear Accelerators with IMRT and IGRT capability, ideal for the treatment of all cancers throughout the body. The Facility also offers patients treatment with Stereotactic Radiosurgery (SRS), Stereotactic Body Radiotherapy (SBRT) and has a Xoft Electronic Brachytherapy Unit (used in the treatment of skin cancer).
