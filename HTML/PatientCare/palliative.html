PALLIATIVE

Palliative care is focused on improving the quality of life of patients and their families through the prevention and relief of suffering resulting from life-threatening illnesses. Radiation Therapy (RT) is an important palliative approach and it is commonly used in a wide number of cancer patients when cure is no longer possible.

UC San Diego Radiation Oncologists are highly experienced in the use of short-course palliative RT, whereby symptoms such as pain, can be effectively addressed with minimal risk of toxicity.

We are committed to utilizing palliative radiation therapy to improve the quality of life in our patients suffering from symptoms due to local and/or metastatic disease

The Department Palliative Team works closely with the Moores Cancer Center Palliative Team to address the needs of both inpatients and outpatients. We are actively developing a novel single day palliative approach greatly reducing the frequency of visits and improving the efficacy of treatment.
