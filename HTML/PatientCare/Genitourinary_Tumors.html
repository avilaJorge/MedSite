Genitourinary Tumors

Genitourinary (GU) tumors include all tumors throughout the genital urinary tract. Many of these tumors, e.g. prostate and bladder cancers, present in older patients, select GU tumors such as testicular cancers may arise in young men.

The treatment of GU tumors is dependent on the disease site, but most involve a combination of surgery, chemotherapy and/or radiation therapy (RT). RT occupies an important role in GU tumors, particularly tumors of the prostate, bladder and testes.

It is important that GU tumor patients are seen and treated at centers with an experienced multi-disciplinary tumor of urologists, medical oncologists and radiation oncologists who specialize in the treatment of these patients.
