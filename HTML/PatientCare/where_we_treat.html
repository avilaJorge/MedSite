WHERE WE TREAT

Radiation Therapy is often a daily treatment delivered over several days to weeks. To reduce its impact on the life of patients and their families, we have strived to offer our services throughout the greater San Diego region, providing our patients access to our physicians and state-of-the-art treatment closer to their homes.
