Our Center

The facility is equipped with a Varian Linear Accelerator with IMRT and IGRT capability, ideal for the treatment of all cancers throughout the body. The Facility also offers patients treatment with Stereotactic Radiosurgery (SRS) and Stereotactic Body Radiotherapy (SBRT) (link IMRT, IGRT, SRS, SBRT to How we treat)
