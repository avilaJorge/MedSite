Gynecologic Cancers

Gynecologic tumors include all cancers of the female genital tract, including tumors of the ovary, uterus, cervix, vagina and vulva. Many gynecologic cancers preferentially affect older patients; however, select tumors, for example cervical cancer, can arise in younger women.

Treatment of gynecologic cancers often involves multiple specialists including Gynecologic Oncologists, Medical Oncologists and Radiation Oncologists. Many patients may require combinations of surgery, chemotherapy and radiation therapy (RT). We are committed to providing our Gynecologic Cancer patients with the latest treatment approaches including IMRT, IGRT, SBRT, and Proton Therapy.

Our Gynecologic Cancer Radiation Oncology are members of the Moores Cancer Center Multi-disciplinary GYN Cancer Treatment Team and work closely with Kaiser Permanente San Diego Medical Oncologists and Surgeons who treat Gynecologic malignancies.

UC San Diego Radiation Oncologists are committed to providing patients access to state-of-the-art clinical trials, many not available elsewhere. Several faculty oversee national cooperative clinical trials using novel treatment approaches, for example a NRG trial evaluating immunotherapy and RT in women with cervical cancer.
