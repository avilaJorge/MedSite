Our Center

The facility is equipped with 4 Varian Linear Accelerator with IMRT and IGRT capability, ideal for the treatment of all cancers throughout the body in both adults and children. The Facility also offers patients treatment with Stereotactic Radiosurgery (SRS) and Stereotactic Body Radiotherapy (SBRT) as well as high-dose rate (HDR) and low-dose rate (HDR) brachytherapy (link IMRT, IGRT, SRS, SBRT, Brachytherapy to How we treat)

Directions

Printable Map and Directions

From Interstate 5: Exit on Genesee Avenue and head east. Turn right on Campus Point Drive and continue past Voigt Drive. At the next intersection, you can turn right toward the Campus Point Parking Structure (across from Sulpizio Cardiovascular Center, on the right) or turn left and continue around the curve to the Athena Parking Structure (on the left).

From I-805: Exit on La Jolla Village Drive and head west. Turn right on Genesee Avenue. Turn left on Campus Point Drive and continue past Voigt Drive. At the next intersection, you can turn right toward the Campus Point Parking Structure (across from Sulpizio Cardiovascular Center, on the right) or turn left and continue around the curve to the Athena Parking Structure (on the left).

Late-night visitors: Between 9 p.m. and 5 a.m., we recommend using I-805 and Regents Road entrances to avoid construction delays along I-5 and Genesee Avenue. Night-time construction activities related to trolley construction are expected to last through 2020. Daytime traffic is not affected.
