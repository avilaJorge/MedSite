HEAD AND NECK CANCERS

Head and Neck Cancers include a large number of malignancies including cancers of the oral cavity, oropharynx and larynx. Included with this group include less common tumors of the salivary glands and thyroid gland.

Head and Neck Cancers are often treated with combinations of surgery, chemotherapy and/or radiation therapy (RT). Treatment should be delivered by an experienced team of ENT surgeons, medical oncologists and radiation oncologists, specializing in the care of these patients. It is also essential that the team be supported by experienced speech/language therapists, diagnostic radiologists, plastic surgeons and pathologists.

We are committed to providing our Head/Neck Cancer patients with the latest treatment approaches including IMRT, IGRT, SBRT, and Proton Therapy.

Our Head/Neck Radiation Oncology specialists are members of the Moores Cancer Center Multi-disciplinary Head/Neck Treatment Team and also work closely with Kaiser Permanente San Diego Medical Oncologists and Surgeons who treat Head/Neck malignancies.
