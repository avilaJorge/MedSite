Chair's Welcome



I would like to personally welcome you to the website of the Department of Radiation Medicine and Applied Sciences at the University of California, San Diego School of Medicine. I am very proud to be a member of this exceptional department, which offers patients access to state-of-the-art therapies delivered in a compassionate, caring environment. The department also has a strong commitment to education and research, particularly in the development and implementation of advanced radiation technologies. Our department is an integral member of Moores Cancer Center, one of only 41 NCI-designated Comprehensive Cancer Centers in the country and the only one in the San Diego region.

This website will give you an overview of our academic department, including research programs and education. In the People section, you will find biographies of our faculty members, complete with their clinical and research focuses and publications. The Research section provides a comprehensive overview of our research programs. Descriptions of our training programs in Radiation Oncology and Medical Physics are included under Education.

If you are a patient or family members looking for an overview of our services, simply click on Patient Care, which will connect you to our pages on the Moores Cancer Center website.

It is my sincere hope that you find our website helpful to you and your family.

Regards,
Arno J. Mundt, MD FACRO FASTRO
Professor and Chair
Radiation Medicine and Applied Sciences
