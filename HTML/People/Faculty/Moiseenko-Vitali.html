Dr. Moiseenko is Technology Service Chief for Genitourinary and Head/Neck Tumors. He also oversees a laboratory within the Center for Translational Radiation Medicine and Imaging.

His research interests include outcomes data analysis, functional imaging, Monte Carlo simulations, propagation of uncertainties associated with radiation therapy and modeling of tumor and normal tissue response to radiation. He is the author of over 90 peer-reviewed journal articles, reviews and book chapters and is a contributor to multiple AAPM Task Group and AAPM/ASTRO Working Group reports.
