Dr. Rahn is a clinical radiation oncologist based at the South Bay Treatment Facility. He is an active member of multiple disease teams including Head & Neck Cancers and CNS/Radiosurgery and oversees the Standards and Guidelines Initiative which sets practice guidelines for all treatment centers.

An experienced clinician, Dr. Rahn has expertise in a wide range of radiotherapy approaches including IMRT, IGRT, SBRT and Cranial SRS. In addition, he has a large experience treating skin cancers with electronic brachytherapy. His research interests include lung SBRT, palliative radiotherapy for pediatric cancers and quality/safety in Radiation Oncology. Dr. Rahn is committed to resident and medical student education and oversees a popular monthly Boards Review Course.
