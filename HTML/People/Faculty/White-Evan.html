Dr. White is a clinical radiation oncologist based at the South Bay Treatment Facility. He is a member of multiple disease teams including GI, lymphoma and Head and Neck malignancies.

He has diverse and extensive clinical experience in the treatment of multiple disease sites, and is adept at managing complex multidisciplinary cancer care and utilizing cutting edge technologies including SRS, SBRT, and IMRT. In addition, he has considerable experience using electronic brachytherapy in the management of skin cancers.
