Dr. Rash is a clinical radiation oncologist based at the Encinitas Treatment Facility. She is an active member of multiple disease teams including Breast Cancers and GI Tumors.

She is an experienced clinical radiation oncologist with expertise in a wide number of techniques including IMRT, SRS, SBRT and intracavitary brachytherapy for breast and gynecologic cancer patients. She is involved with resident education and oversees a course devoted to the management of acute and chronic radiation toxicities.
