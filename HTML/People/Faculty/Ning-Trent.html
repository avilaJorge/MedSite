Dr. Ning is the Technology Service Chief for Hematologic Malignancies and oversees the Total Body and Total Skin Irradiation programs.

In addition to his work on clinical physics, Dr. Ning has an interest in hospital radiation safety and linac shielding. He is also interested in radiation biophysics and the radiotherapy and the immune system.
