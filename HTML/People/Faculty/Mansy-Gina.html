Dr. Mansy is the Medical Director of the South Bay Treatment Center and also serves as the Liaison Director with Kaiser Permanente.

Dr. Mansy main clinical interests focus on breast and gynecologic malignancies. She is an active member of the RMAS Breast Cancer Treatment Team and participates in multiple Kaiser Permanente Cancer Committees. Twice named a Top Doctor by the San Diego Magazine, she is also an experienced in brachytherapy approaches in patients with gynecologic malignancies including cervical and uterine cancers.
