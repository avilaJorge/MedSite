Dr. Sanghvi is the Director of the Division of Clinical Radiation Oncology and oversees all the clinical radiation oncologists in the Department. He is the Hematologic Malignancies Section Chief and serves on several disease teams, including Head & Cancers and CNS Tumors.

Dr. Sanghvi has considerable expertise using multiple treatment technologies including IMRT, IGRT, proton therapy and stereotactic radiosurgery (SRS) in patients with head/neck and CNS tumors. He oversees the multidisciplinary Lymphoma Tumor Board and has developed novel delivery techniques using deep-inspirational breath hold (DIBH) to minimize potential toxicities in lymphoma patients undergoing mediastinal irradiation. He treats patients at both the La Jolla Facility and the Proton Center.

In addition to his clinical responsibilities, Dr. Sanghvi serves on the UC San Diego School of Medicine Recruitment and Admissions Executive Committee, the Medical School Standards and Promotions Committee, the Medical Staff Executive Committee and is co-chair of the Radiation Oncology Disease Team within the Moores Cancer Center Cabinet.
