CARL ROSSI, MD

PROFESSOR

UCSD PROFILE
  • Birthplace | Anaheim, California
  • BA Biology | Claremont McKenna College 1984
  • MD | Loyola University 1988
  • Residency | Loma Linda University 1992
  • Clinical Focus | Prostate, Protons
Dr. Rossi is the Medical Director of the Scripps Proton Treatment Center and oversees the Prostate Cancer Proton Program.

Dr. Rossi is an internationally-known expert in the treatment of prostate and other malignancies with proton therapy. He is a frequent invited speaker at conferences in the United States, Asia and Europe, and has published in numerous respected oncology journals including the Journal of Clinical Oncology, Urology and the Journal of the American Medical Association (JAMA). He was a co-investigator on the highly influential randomized proton-photon prostate cancer clinical trial conducted at both Harvard and Loma Linda, which was subsequently published in JAMA. A dedicated clinician, Dr. Rossi has been recognized as a Top Doctor by US World News & Reports.
