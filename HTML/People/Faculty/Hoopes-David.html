UCSD PROFILE
  • Birthplace | Tucson, Arizona
  • BS Molecular Biology | Brigham Young University 1997
  • MD | Uniformed Services University of the Health Sciences 2002
  • Residency | Indiana University 2007
  • Clinical Focus | Prostate, Head/Neck, General Radiation Oncology
Dr. Hoopes is the Medical Director of the 4S Ranch Treatment Facility and an active member of multiple disease teams including GU Malignancies and Gynecologic Cancers.

His research interests include patient involvement in decision making, hypofractionated and stereotactic techniques and radiation safety/quality assurance. His quality assurance work has included time as a site surveyor for the American College of Radiology Practice Accreditation Program, membership on the ASTRO Health Services Research Committee and an appointment to the Radiation Oncology Incident Learning System (RO-ILS), a system designed to share, analyze and learn from radiation incidents worldwide.
