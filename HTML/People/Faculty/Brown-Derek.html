Dr. Brown is an Associate Director of the Division of Medical Physics & Technology and is the Program Director of the Medical Physics Residency Program. In addition, he is the Director of the UCSD Learning Center.

Dr. Brown is an active clinician and educator. He has a strong interest in Global Health Care and is involved with the organizations Radiating Hope and TreatSafely which are dedicated to improving access to modern radiotherapy throughout the world as well as to developing practice educational materials.
