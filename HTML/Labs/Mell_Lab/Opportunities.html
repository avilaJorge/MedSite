Opportunities

Post-Doctoral Fellowships

Our lab periodically funds 2-year post-doctoral fellowships depending on hiring needs and available research funding. Individuals looking for such a position with the appropriate skill set may contact our Division's Administrative Assistant, Lailani Aguila

Visiting Scholarships

We invite interest from both domestic and foreign visiting scholars for fellowships in our lab. Funding for such positions is typically not available, however, if your host institution is able to help support a position, we accept scholars for durations of 6-24 months.

Medical Students

UCSD Medical Students interested in joining our research program are welcome to contact Lailani Aguila to request a meeting. Past medical students have done summer or ISP projects, often funded by scholarships. UCSD Students interested in a year-long research scholarship under the NIH T32 grant should contact Carolyn Kelly.

Foreign and domestic medical students are invited to apply for a research position. Funding for such positions is typically not available, however, if your host institution is able to help support a position, we accept scholars for durations of 3-24 months.

Prospective Clinical Research Coordinators and Undergraduates

Those planning on a career in medicine or allied health professions who are interested in a position as a clinical trial coordinator should contact Erica Burbano. Undergraduates who are looking to shadow physician-scientists should contact Lailani Aguila
