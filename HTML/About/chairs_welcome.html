Moores Cancer Center

UC San Diego Moores Cancer Center (MCC) is one of just 50 NCI-Designated Comprehensive Cancer Centers in the country and the only one in the greater San Diego region. Comprised of over 230 clinicians and researchers including 16 National Academy of Science members, MCC has over $140M in Extra-Mural Grant Funding and its faculty publish over 1000 journal articles each year.

 

UCSD Medical School

Founded in 1960, UC San Diego occupies over 2000 acres along the beautiful California coastline. Comprised of approximately 1400 faculty, the University offers over 200 undergraduate and graduate programs, enrolling nearly 30,000 undergraduate and 8500 graduate students. UC San Diego School of Medicine includes over 475 medical students and 780 residents (including 16 RMAS residents). School of Medicine faculty are ranked #1 in national and federal research funding per faculty.
