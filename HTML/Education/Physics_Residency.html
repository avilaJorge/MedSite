Medical Physics Residency

Welcome to the UC San Diego Radiation Medicine and Applied Sciences Medical Physics Residency Program. The program was inaugurated on July 1, 2007. We are accredited by the Commission on Accrediation of Medical Physics Education Programs (CAMPEP). We currently accept two applicants each year.

TRAINING THE FUTURE

Our goal is to train the next generation of medical physics leaders. Through clinical excellence, cutting edge research, and building independent professional relationships with patients, our physics residents will guide the future of radiotherapy physics.
