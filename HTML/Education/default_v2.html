Education

The UC San Diego Department of Radiation Medicine and Applied Sciences has a strong commitment to education and training. The Department sponsors two accredited residency programs in Radiation Oncology and Medical Physics. Multiple Department faculty are also involved with medical student education and oversee clinical rotations as well as a variety of research opportunities.
